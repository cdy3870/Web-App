Rent Items for Your Use!

How our renting system works

To rent items from other users...

Simply search for the item you need using our search functions or search through categories and see what kinds of items people are renting out! In order to rent, you must have a payment method linked to your profile--don't worry though! You will only ever be charged if you are renting an item out. This ensures that any damage caused to the item is covered and that the renter is getting compensated for renting out their item!


Rent Your Items Out to Others!

How our system works

To rent your own item out for others to use...

Click the "Upload" link at the top of the page and fill out some information about your item. Try to be as descriptive and accurate with your descriptions as possible! You can set a price to rent out the item, but please try to be reasonable! Nobody is going to rent out an item that they could buy for cheaper. And while your item is being rented by somebody, you will receive payment for your item. The renters credit card and payment information is stored so in case anything happens to your item, full compensation can be paid upon investigation!
